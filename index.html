A blue rose is a flower of the genus Rosa (family Rosaceae) that presents blue-to-violet In some cases, blue roses represent a gift to one's true love, although this tends to be a message sent by a couple who are already in a romantic relationship. Due to the unnatural nature of blue roses and solemn color, darker variants represent love this is unrequited, hence the sadness associated with the color and association roses have with love. Light blue roses, however, refer to sentimental love, a message that tends to be platonic in nature and more used to show a strong appreciation for a distant loved one.
